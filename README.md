# Business Performance Analytics for a Canadian Consulting Company using SQL Server

## Executive Summary
This project focuses on analyzing **key business performance metrics for a Canadian consulting services company** in order to improve operational efficiency and support strategic decision-making. The organization provides specialized training and consulting courses designed to help small businesses in Canada enhance their operations and grow sustainably.
To support better data-driven decision-making, a structured database solution was developed using SQL Server Management Studio (SSMS). The system was designed to store, manage, and analyze business data related to courses, instructors, customers, inquiries, and financial performance.
Using a Business Intelligence (BI) approach, the project implements a set of analytical SQL queries to extract key performance indicators (KPIs) that are critical for evaluating the organization’s business performance. 
By leveraging the analytical capabilities of SSMS, the organization gains the ability to transform raw operational data into meaningful insights. This enables management to make informed strategic decisions, optimize resource allocation, improve customer engagement, and support long-term sustainable growth.

 
## Business Objectives
**1. Optimize Revenue and Profitability**
The project analyzes revenue, cost, and profit generated by each course. This helps the organization identify high-performing courses and make better decisions regarding pricing, course offerings, and resource allocation.

**2. Improve Customer Conversion Rates**
A conversion rate analysis was conducted to measure how many inquiries successfully convert into customers. Understanding conversion rates helps evaluate marketing effectiveness and identify opportunities to improve customer acquisition.

**3. Improve Course and Instructor Management**
Instructor engagement with courses is analyzed to ensure efficient resource utilization and balanced workload distribution. This helps the organization manage instructor assignments more effectively.

**4. Ensure Compliance and Operational Flexibility**
The system allows instructor work-hour constraints to be adjusted based on changes in Canadian employment regulations. This ensures the organization remains compliant with government policies while maintaining operational efficiency.

**5. Maintain Data Integrity and Efficient Data Management**
The database structure ensures accurate and well-organized data storage. This enables reliable reporting, faster data retrieval, and improved decision-making.
 

## Analysis Insights:

**Course Cost Analysis for Budget Management:**
This analysis evaluates the cost associated with each course and identifies the most and least expensive programs. Understanding course costs allows managers to monitor budget allocation and ensure that financial resources are used efficiently.
By identifying high-cost courses, management can review operational expenses and adjust pricing strategies if necessary. Similarly, recognizing lower-cost courses helps determine opportunities where profit margins can potentially be increased.
This insight supports better budget planning and cost control while ensuring that course offerings remain financially sustainable.

**Course Demand Analysis**
This analysis ranks courses based on the number of inquiries received from potential customers. The number of inquiries serves as an indicator of market interest and demand for each course.
By identifying courses with the highest inquiry volumes, managers can focus marketing efforts and promotional campaigns on programs that attract strong customer interest. It also helps identify courses with lower demand that may require improved marketing strategies or repositioning.
Understanding course demand helps the organization align its offerings with market needs and prioritize high-interest training programs.

**Conversion Rate Analysis**
This analysis measures the conversion rate from inquiries to enrolled customers for each course. The conversion rate provides insight into how effectively customer inquiries are converted into actual course registrations.
This information allows managers to evaluate the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and inquiry follow-up processes. Courses with higher conversion rates indicate strong customer interest and effective engagement, while lower conversion rates may signal opportunities for improving communication or marketing strategies.
These insights help optimize customer acquisition efforts and improve overall enrollment rates.

**Instructor Allocation Across Courses**
This analysis examines the number of instructors assigned to each course. Understanding instructor distribution helps managers ensure that courses are adequately staffed and that instructor workloads remain balanced.
Courses with fewer instructors may require additional staffing to maintain teaching quality, while courses with higher instructor availability may allow for better scheduling flexibility.
This insight supports effective human resource planning and ensures consistent course delivery quality.

**Resource Utilization Analysis**
The resource utilization rate measures how effectively instructor hours are used in relation to course demand. This metric compares the number of instructor hours allocated to each course with the level of customer interest and enrollment.
Courses with high utilization indicate efficient use of instructor resources, while low utilization may suggest that instructor time is underused.
This analysis helps managers optimize instructor scheduling, adjust course offerings when necessary, and improve the overall efficiency of resource allocation.

**Customer Churn Monitoring**
This analysis tracks customers who are no longer active with the organization by updating their status to “No.” Maintaining accurate customer status helps the company better understand customer retention and churn patterns.
Identifying customers who leave the company allows managers to analyze possible reasons for disengagement and develop retention strategies. It also ensures that marketing efforts and communications focus primarily on active customers.
This insight improves customer relationship management and supports more effective business planning.
 
**Inquiry Status Tracking for Operational Efficiency**
The inquiry management system categorizes customer inquiries into three statuses: Pending, In Progress, and Completed. This classification allows the organization to track the progress of each inquiry throughout the customer engagement process.
By clearly monitoring inquiry status, managers can ensure that customer requests are addressed in a timely manner and that no inquiries are overlooked. It also helps prioritize follow-ups and improve response efficiency.
This structured tracking system enhances operational organization and strengthens customer service management.
